Direct Proof

claim: if $P$ then $Q$

proof: Suppose $P$ then $Q$

claim: if $m$ and $n$ are odd then $mn$ is odd

proof: Suppose that $m$ and $n$ are odd . 

Thus: $m=2x+1$ and $n=2y+1$ for $x,y\in \mathbb{Z}$

Notice that: $mn=(2x+1)(2y+1) = 4xy+2x+2y+1$ then $mn$ is odd
